Koolnect 4

Koolnect 4 (definitely not connect 4) is a game where 2 players take turns placing coloured circular tiles in a grid. the goal of the game is to connect 4 tiles you can do this horizontally vertically or diagonally.

Amogus Minesweeper

in Minesweeper bombs are placed randomly around a grid and the goal is to correctly identify the locations of the bombs without accidentally blowing one up. numbers will appear indicating how many bombs are in the radius around it. this radius includes the left right top bottom and diagonals around the number.
